      My name is Toadsnout, and I always wanted a dragon. Also, I wanted a legendary dragon that is really strong. So I decided to tame the dragon that only existed in legends. I meant the dragon Stormfire. Stormfire was a dragon with black scales and white streaks. It had sky blue crystals on its back and wings. It had had jagged teeth, a loud roar, and yellow eyes.
 Its skills were its bite force, its blue fire, its ice breathing power, and its power to summon in lightning. Its lightning strike could kill 1000000000 cows in one strike! So, I decided to search for one. Since i was strong and slim, the citizens liked me a lot and cheered for me. However, I was actually thinking how to go to its region. These dragons live in the core of a volcano, which is why these dragons are so rare and very hard to reach. No one ever tamed a Stormfire dragon, for the dragon's blue fire might burn anyone and the dragon's ice breath can make a person frozen alive.
