When my father gave me permission to go on the quest, I borrowed my dad's boat, the Black Bird. On my way, I saw giant rocks shaped like a dragon, an island shaped like a skull. There was blue smoke coming out from the right eye of the skull-shaped like island. It seemed like the soul was coming out of a skull. When I arrived at the island, I walked inside the mouth of the  mouth of the skull. It felt like a scorching day in the summer even though it was winter! When I arrived at dragon's layer(the core of the island), the dragon was eating 10 cows at once. I sneaked up behind the dragon, and saw a dragon egg! I quietly picked it up, and tried to leave. However, the dragon saw me, and chased me towards the entrance of the place. Because of the dragon's size, it wasn't able to come out. I was the only person who escaped from a Stormfire dragon. It was time to make it hatch.
