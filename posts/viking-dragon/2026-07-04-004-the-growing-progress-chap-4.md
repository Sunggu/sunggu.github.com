The next day, I decided to train my dragon. At first, this dragon did not listen to me. I remembered that a dragon needs to be trusted to be tamed. So, I decided to give it a bath in the lake. I suddenly heard a rumbling sound from my dragon's stomach. It meant that it was very hungry. So I decided to give it some cod, salmon, and some mackerel. It's colorful crystals suddenly started to glow, and the crystals became a mint color, and unlike its species, it had white scales! I knew that this dragon might be stronger than its family! Also the dragon became big enough to ride. So, I tried to ride it, but it shook me off. It meant it didn't trust me a lot. So, I waited for it to trust me.
