## Chapter 3
 
When I came back to my house, I examined the egg carefully. The egg had blue crystals all arund, it also had a black color, and white streaks.I wondered how to make it hatch. You might say I should read a scroll, but I was the first person to collect a Stormfire egg and I needed to figure it out myself. I first tried to put it inside water. However,it didn't work. Also, I decided to put it in fire. That did not work, either.

I just decided to put it outside. Suddenly, rain fell, and a lightning bolt was aiming towards the egg! I tried to move it out of the way, but I was too late. However, when the lightning bolt hit the egg, the egg hatched! The baby dragon saw me and recognized me as its master.
